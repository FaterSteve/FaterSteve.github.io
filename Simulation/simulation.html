I finished the bonding simulation I was working on. The Ionic bonding simulation is interactive, while the Covalent bonding simulation is not. I am not sure if the Chromebooks are powerful enough to run the simulation or if the school internet would block it, so I figured I would post it here to play with if you feel like it. Note that the Ionic bonding simulation starts paused and that the sounds are annoying.

Controls for Ionic bonding Simulation:
Pause/Unpause - P
Movement - WASD or Arrow Keys
Mouse Sensitivity Control - Shift(decreases) Ctrl(increases)
Reset Simulation - 1
Change Simulation - 2
Summon Cation - Left Mouse Click or Z
Summon Antion - Right Mouse Click or X
Movement Speed Control - Mouse Scroll Wheel

Controls for Covalent bonding Simulation:
Reset Simulation - 2
Change Simulation - 1

There is unfortunately not controller or touch support.
